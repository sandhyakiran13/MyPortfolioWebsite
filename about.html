A Little About Me

My name is Sandhyakiran Saroj, and I hold a degree in Psychology and English Literature from Mumbai University. I completed my schooling at a government school in Goregaon, where I had the privilege of being taught by Teach For India (TFI) fellows. Their innovative and transformative approach to teaching showed me that learning could be both engaging and life-changing. Inspired by their unwavering dedication and the profound impact they had on my life, I chose to join the TFI fellowship after graduating.

What inspired Me to be a Fellow?

This is a picture of me holding a quilling card given to me by my TFI teacher appreciate my hardwork in my classroom, later quilling became one of my hobbies. I am deeply grateful for the guidance of Teach For India fellows, who showed me that school and learning can be enjoyable. This experience inspired me to pursue the Teach For India fellowship.
