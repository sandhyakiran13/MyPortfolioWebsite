About Me

About Me Title

As a Psychology and English Literature graduate, I am deeply curious about human behavior and the factors influencing actions. This curiosity has shaped my belief in the transformative power of education and inspired me to join the Teach For India fellowship. Growing up in an underprivileged background, I’ve experienced firsthand how impactful guidance and quality education that I got from my TFI fellows can change lives, and I am committed to giving back by creating similar opportunities for my students.
