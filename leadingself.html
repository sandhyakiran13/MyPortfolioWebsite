Leading Self

My Reflections

Building the skills and mindset to provide quality and equitable education - When I began the fellowship, my understanding of lesson planning was basic. Over time, I have made significant progress and now place myself at a 3-3.5 level (out of 5) in the LIT pillars: Connect, Envision, Plan, Execute, and Reflect. I see challenges as opportunities for growth, both in skills and mindset. Through training in the Leadership in Teaching framework and content-focused sessions in Literacy and Social Sciences, I have developed a certain level of ability to design purposeful and impactful lesson plans using the 5 Magic Questions framework, focusing on purpose, integration, mastery, methods, and partnerships. I have grown into an educator who places students at the center of every decision, prioritizing their needs, growth, and holistic development. Leading India

Photo with Shaheen Mistri

Over the past two years, I have interacted with multiple donors, along with Shaheen herself and the Development Team, by sharing my story and understanding of education. I have represented thousands of student alumni on such platforms, highlighting the positive impact of TFI fellows on students. I also shared ways donors could support education initiatives effectively, showcasing the importance of storytelling, advocacy, and relationship-building.

Photo of Me at a Roundtable Discussion

I was invited by TFI Mumbai's recruitment team to share my fellowship journey with aspiring fellows. During this session, I highlighted what it takes to become a fellow, what a typical day looks like, and the challenges and rewards of the role. This experience enhanced my skills in public speaking, mentorship, and inspiring others through authentic storytelling.

Internship Experience

I strongly value partnerships between adults and students and believe in amplifying student voices. During the break between Year-1 and Year-2 of my fellowship, I interned with TFI on projects related to communications and outreach. My role involved strategic communication and community-building, where I focused on connecting student alumni with meaningful opportunities like being part of the Student Advisory Board.

ASB Teacher Academy 2023-24

I participated in the ASB Teacher Academy 2023-24, where I advanced my understanding of pedagogy, effective teaching practices, and classroom structures that foster better student learning. Through this program, I interacted with educators and leaders from various organizations, learning their best practices in education and leadership, which helped refine my approach to teaching.
